Privacy Policy

Agreeable is designed to provide functionality without ever collecting personal information.

Information We Access

Agreeable has access via Chrome Extension to the following:

  • Detecting Your Active Tab
  • Accessing and managing extension settings via Chrome Storage
  • Reading Active Page Content

All of these permissions have been reviewed and approved by the Chrome Web Store.

Third-Party Services

The Agreeable extension does not use any third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising services. Your usage remains private and local to your device.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

By using Agreeable, you acknowledge that your privacy is protected and that no personal information is collected.
